Who we are ?

Pet India Industries is a pet bottle manufacturing company and it was established in the year 2000 , under the guidance of “Mr. T. Raj Singh” who has experience of more than 20 years in the industry. We have been scaling new heights in our business endeavour. In fact, we are counted amidst most prominent Plastic PET Products Manufacturers, Suppliers and Exporters from India.

What we do ?

The company makes use technologically advanced machinery and methods for unblemished production of Plastic PET Products. Customer satisfaction is foreseen as the definitive aim of the company. Therefore, we cater to meet the exact requirements of the customers in the most proficient manner.

Based on the same, the company has nurtured long term business alliance with the clients and takes all necessary steps to exceed their expectations. Consequently, the company is considered to be one of the trustworthy Plastic PET Products Manufacturers, Suppliers and Exporters from India.
